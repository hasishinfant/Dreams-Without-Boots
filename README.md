# 🌍 The Story

**1950.**

India qualifies for the FIFA World Cup.

But history ends there.

Or does it?

**Dreams Without Boots** imagines a world where an entire nation refused to give up.

Farmers donate their harvest.

Mothers sacrifice their jewelry.

Shoemakers stitch boots through the night.

Children hand over their only football.

An entire country sends its team to Brazil...

And history changes forever.

This is not a recreation.

This is the story of the future that could have been.
